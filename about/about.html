About

The Team

The K4SM Game Development Team is a student founded and maintained organization; learning the latest technologies in school in order to make an impact outside of school.

Our Mission

The K4SM Game Development Team strives to build perfectly unique games, both in design and development with the dream of being able to make a name for ourselves in the ever-flourishing game industry.

Our Philosophy

The K4SM Game Development Team is a fun-loving group of three with the firm belief that in order to make enjoyable games, we need to be able to have fun while making.
