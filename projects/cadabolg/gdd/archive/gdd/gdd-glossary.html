P

Player: A human that plays the game. Player Party: A group of three Actors from the Player's Crew that participates in Battles. Group consists of Leader, Ally 1 and Ally 2 which are assigned by the Player before a Battle. After each Round, the Actors in the Player's party can be swapped. Enemy Party: A group of five Actors from the Enemy's Crew that participates in Battles. Group consists of Leader, Enemy1, Enemy2, Enemy3, Enemy4, EnemyNext which are generated from the Enemy Cast. If Enemy1, Enemy2, Enemy3, Enemy4 fall in battle, they are replaced by EnemyNext. After each Round, the Actors in the Enemy Party may be swapped.

[top]

Q

Quest: A scripted series of Battles, the Player must complete.
