8.3 Plot

Arc 1: Legend of Heroes


The player follows eight stories. These are narrated in second person.

The Dead of Night
The Head Monk of the Blue Moon Temple and his bodyguards Lei, Rai and Jin are on a pilgrimage to visit the Shrines of Nature Gods all over Xian. When going through the Forest of Cherry Blossom, their progress is stalled by a heavy rainstorm. They seek shelter in a nearby Castle. Later that night, the inhabitants of the Castle turn into Spectres and the lord of the Castle kidnaps the Head Monk to sacrifice for a ritual. Lei must fight the Spectres, rescue the Head Monk and break the curse of the Castle.
