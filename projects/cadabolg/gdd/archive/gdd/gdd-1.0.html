1.1 Game Overview

Cadabolg is a 2D action role-playing game for the PC that puts players in fast paced, arena style battles with a highly customizable party of characters fight to against waves of enemies and take on insane boss fights! Battles yield a lot of different loot allowing players to mix and match theirs characters' development to their needs. Cadabolg aims to bring back the style of action role-playing seen in games such as Terranigma, Seiken Densetsu 3 and the Tales of series without the hindarances common to most JRPGs such as grinding, random encounters, long cutscenes, backtracking and stereotypical characters.
